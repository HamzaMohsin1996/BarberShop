Overview of Next Level Academy and what separates them from the rest

Next Level Barber Academy was established in 2018, and is proudly acclaimed as the first and only barber school in Gaston County.

It was created through the vision of Justin Marlow, who is a Gastonia native, and licensed master barber for 15 years.

Mr. Marlow's premise for founding this academy was to help create jobs for the community, and to provide a creative platform for both men and women to showcase their artistic abilities through the art of barbering.
